ABOUT US

WE HELP PEOPLE ACROSS THE GLOBE

The work of the Save Lives Foundation, which began with helping refugees find new homes, has evolved into multiple endeavors across different fields.

We have representative offices all over the world and work with international organizations.

Our main activities are humanitarian aid, assistance to refugees, and fundraising.
