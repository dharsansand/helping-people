Have Any Questions?

Feel free to leave a message or check the section below. We'll be glad to help you with any issue.

Where does the foundation get money from?

The Save Lives foundation is based on donations from individuals and companies. We are constantly working on attracting new sources of funding.

In which countries does the foundation operate?

We operate in over 20 countries across various continents.

How to become a volunteer?

You can sign up through our website or visit any of our local offices to learn more about volunteering opportunities.

Where can I bring humanitarian aid?

We have drop-off locations in major cities. Please check our website for the nearest location.

Is there a minimum donation amount?

There is no minimum donation amount. Every contribution counts!
