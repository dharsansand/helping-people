About

SAVE LIVES HELPS REFUGEES AND VICTIMS OF WAR

We do everything possible and impossible so that everyone can get the help they need.

The Save Lives charity foundation operates worldwide. Our volunteers go to war zones, places of natural disasters, and unstable regions to help people. We deliver humanitarian aid and find new homes for refugees.

Save Lives has been operating since 2015 and provides fundraising and goods collection, procurement, and delivery of humanitarian aid to the right spots.
